# shearwaters-climate

Shearwater seabird sightings and climate variables off the coast of California.

Is there a climate diven shift in seasonal timing of seabird appearances to breeding areas for Sooty and Pink-footed Shearwaters, using eBird sighting data and NOAA weather data off the coast of California.

ebird: sightings in Bodega and Monterey Bays
NOAA: temp, wind, sst, upwelling index, enso index

Group by month and year and identify shifts in peak timing, duration of prescence, and overall counts in the last 10 years (since 2015). 

Skills used: time series modelling, visualization, statistical modeling

R - ggplot2, seaborn?, Rshiny, Leaflet? (heat map_

Final product - Rshiny app and webpage
